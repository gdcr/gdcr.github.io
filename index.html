What is Gopher Day of Coderetreat?

Thursday, January 12th 2017
8:30am - 5:00pm
Bruininks Hall, Room 330
Free
Breakfast and Lunch provided
Happy hour afterwards

Gopher Day of Coderetreat is a free, U of M sponsored coderetreat open to everyone. We spend the day working on small problems and "focusing on the fundamentals of software development and design." Programmers world wide have found that dedicating a day to practicing their skills has made their code cleaner and easier to change.

Can I Attend?

Yes! Gopher Day of Coderetreat is open to everyone. You do not need to be a U of M employee or student. All programming languages and experience levels welcome. You only need to be excited about spending a day improving your programming craft!

Code of Conduct

What Will I Do?

Practice

You will spend the day working with others, discussing and solving small programming problems. The problems will change, encouraging you to explore other approaches.

Learn

You will work with different programmers throughout the day. Learn about the languages they use, their problem solving techniques, and their handy tricks!

Teach

You will share your knowledge with programmers from across the Twin Cities. It doesn't matter how long you've been programming, you'll know something that they don't!
